Icon Creation Guide

To complete your extension, you need to create three icon sizes: 16x16, 48x48, and 128x128 pixels.

Instructions:

  1. Create a folder named "icons" in your extension directory
  2. Find a suitable airplane or aviator-themed icon online (ensure it's free to use)
  3. Resize it to 16x16, 48x48, and 128x128 pixels
  4. Save them as icon16.png, icon48.png, and icon128.png in the icons folder

Once you have created these icon files, your extension will be ready to load into your browser.
